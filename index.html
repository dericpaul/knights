We also undertake Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC?s) for all of the above systems for Commercial Premises, Industrial Premises, Manufacturing units, Residential Buildings, Hotels, Hospitals etc. Keystone Fire Protection Sysytem specializes in complete project consulting, design, supply, installation, testing and commissioning for the following systems :

    CO2 Fire Suppression System

    CO2 fire suppression system eliminates the oxygen to suppress the fire when the suppression system detects smoke or fire.

    Fire Alarm Systems (Conventional/Addressable)

    A fire alarm system warns people when smoke, fire or other fire-related particles are detected, these alarms may be activated automatically from smoke and heat detectors or manually.

    Fire Detection System

    Fire detection system uses sensors to tell there is smoke in the air.

    Sprinkler System

    It consist of a water supply system, providing adequate pressure and flowrate to a water distribution piping system , onto which fire sprinklers are connected.

    Water Spray System (HVW/MVW)

    Water spray systems are permanently installed systems that take out fires through water sprayed from interconnected nozzles, it can be activated manually or automatically.

    Fire Hydrant System

    A fire hydrant is a connection point by which firefighters can tap into a water supply.

    Gas Detector System

    It is a device that detects the presence of gases in an area, It can be used to detect combustible , flammable and toxic gases.

    Public Address System

    A public address system is an electronic amplification system with a mixer, amplifier and loudspeakers used to reinforce a sound source.

    Wet Riser System

    A wet riser is a supply system intended to distribute water to multiple levels or compartments of a building.
